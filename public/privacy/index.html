Privacy Policy

Auren Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

Auren is a premium iPhone finance app designed to help users track transactions, goals, subscriptions, budgets, places, analytics, and financial habits.

Information We Collect

When you use Auren, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and display name.
  • Profile information, such as an optional profile image.
  • Financial tracking data you choose to enter, including transactions, goals, subscriptions, budgets, liked items, and net worth items.
  • Support messages you send through email, in-app support, or live chat.
  • Location information when you allow location access for the Places feature.
  • Purchase and subscription status provided through Apple StoreKit.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Create, secure, and sync your Auren account.
  • Display your dashboard, analytics, transactions, goals, subscriptions, budgets, and settings.
  • Help you find nearby places when you choose to use location-based features.
  • Provide support and respond to your requests.
  • Manage premium subscription access.
  • Improve app reliability, safety, and user experience.

Data Storage

Auren uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage to provide account login, cloud sync, profile images, and app data storage. Your app data is stored under your user account and separated from other users.

Live Chat and Support

Auren may use third-party support tools, including tawk.to, to provide live chat and customer support. Information you send through live chat may be processed by that service so we can respond to your request.

Location Data

Auren only uses your location when you allow it and when it is relevant to features such as finding nearby places. You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions and Payments

Auren subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers only as needed to operate Auren, including Firebase, Apple StoreKit, and customer support tools.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and app data from within Auren or by contacting us at support@auren.app. Deleting your Auren account removes your Auren profile and synced app data. App Store subscriptions must be managed separately through your Apple account.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no system is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children

Auren is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Financial Disclaimer

Auren is a personal finance tracking and planning tool. Auren is not a bank, lender, broker, investment advisor, tax advisor, or legal advisor. Auren does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@auren.app.
